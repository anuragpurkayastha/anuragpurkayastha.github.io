Guess the colour!

In this game, you will try to guess the colour given by the (r,g,b) code.

Instructions are as follows:

  1. A set of RGB values will be displayed.
  2. Six different colours will be displayed.
  3. You must guess which of the 6 colours matches the given RGB value.
